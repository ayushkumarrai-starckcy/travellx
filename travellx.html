Travel Terminology: -

Backpacking
A form of low-cost, independent travel that involves carrying all necessary belongings in a backpack and staying in budget accommodations.
Itinerary
A detailed plan or route of a journey, including dates, times, places to visit, and activities to do during the trip.
Jet Lag
A temporary sleep disorder that occurs when traveling across multiple time zones, causing fatigue and disorientation due to disruption of the body's internal clock.
